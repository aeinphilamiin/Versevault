Hope 🕊

Fields are empty, the land dries
Seeking a bird’s nest, none is found
Leaves are withered, greenery has faded
The Sun blazes at its peak.


Travellers feel tired
Farmers yearn for rain
No birds or animals are seen
They move in their burrows and holes.


Cranes fly around in search of prey
Tired of flying around
The merry children are back from school
But there’s no place for them to play.


They run to the field and play
The sun moves back toward the horizon
It slowly descends.
Everyone moves back to their homes.


The same happens the next day
They wake with the hope of rain.
They see a tiny drop of rain
What bliss to see and enjoy!


It transforms the life of everything.
And everything comes alive.
