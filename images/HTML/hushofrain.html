Hush of Rain

Touching softly on the window pane,
Cooling the air with a gentle breeze,
I long to watch a little longer,
As it glides with quiet ease.


I ponder how the days once were—
Rainy hours and chilling memories,
Playing in puddles, folding paper boats,
Watching them drift with the teasing breeze.


Sitting alone with my little pup,
Gently patting its fluffy fur,
My eyes rest upon a favorite book,
While thoughts within begin to stir.


The world outside and the heart within
Blend in a calm, unspoken way,
And in the hush of rain and chill,
I find a peace that chooses to stay.
