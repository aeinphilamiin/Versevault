⸙ My Companion

Crawls here and there with me,
Stretches and plays with me,
Moves with me wherever I go,
As a cute companion of mine.


Hears my voices and whispers,
Chuckles softly by my side,
Reminds me that I’m not lonely,
Serves as a true friend.


The one who shares everything with me -
My companion, my shadow.
