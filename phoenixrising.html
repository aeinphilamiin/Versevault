PHOENIX RISING

Emerges with a full spirit
From an unfathomable source deep within
He struggled a lot and endured the hardest trials
Facing moments that seemed close to death.

Took time to heal the wound
Those deep scars that were hard to mend
His limbs and arms waited to move again
Filled with renewed vigour and strength.

With an unyielding will to rise
Emerged like a phoenix from the ashes
At last the moment arrived—
To break free from every struggle.

Many thought he would not survive
Yet prayers were offered with heartfelt devotion.
God heard the cries of his loved ones,
Those who stood by him with hope and faith.

Everyone longed to see him return,
God answered their prayers.
A glimpse of hope appeared—
The precious hope of life.

The life for which everyone longed,
Longing to dream, longing to live,
To cherish the fruits of true love,
And embrace the beauty of life again.
