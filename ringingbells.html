RINGING BELLS 🔔

The bell rings, the lessons begin.
Students sit in their places,
Busy with work, play and fun
The learning hub, a place of fun.


Teachers come and go,
Giving their daily lessons.
Not bothered about what to learn,
Thinking they’ve plenty of time.


They celebrate the best part of life,
Chatting with friends and creating lively moments.
Some are busy with their work
But others tease them, calling them, ‘learning mugs’.


They enjoy imitating their teachers,
And sometimes pass exams with naughty chits
Finally, the day arrives
To face the real phase of life.


No sleep, no phones, no friends-
They realize, with pain, they are on their own.
Regretting their past habits,
They decide to change, work hard day and night.


At last, they enjoy the fruit of hard work,
The best reward of all.
