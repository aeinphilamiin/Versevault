Seek Within

Time starts—get ready to move on
To the world we live in,
Where gifts are offered,
Waiting to choose the best.

Still confused about where to move on,
Yet ready to move with courage.
Leaps to the top of a hill,
Tries, but fails,
And continues to move ahead.

To the summit within—the goal to achieve,
Hard to reach,
Yet moving ahead, seeking hope.
Still confused about where to move on,
Ponders twice before taking a leap.

We rise and we fall
With a heart full of cheer.
Pushing to reach the pinnacle
Of life, waiting to greet us with hope.
