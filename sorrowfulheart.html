Sorrowful Heart

Echoes of words, harsh words,
Hard to listen to, hard to bear.
Stings like a sharpened knife
Hurts and pains forever.


No soothing words to comfort,
The heart aches with its harshness.
Pierces every inch of the heart,
Enters like an uninvited guest.


Anger rises from the pain,
Making it strong and powerful.
Learns to survive, learns to thrive.
Fire melts and burns like fuel.


The hurt heart melts,
Into something strange!
