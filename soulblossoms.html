Soul Blossoms

The little stars sparkle with light
Bringing rays of joy into my life
Your warm chats and treasured love
Make my heart a happy place.

Your voices, like sweet chatter
Are a delight to hear
You are my precious gems
Shining bright and clear.

Continue your journey with your dreams
Your thoughts and unique theme.
You light up the world within,
My beloved daughters.

May your path be filled with colours of joy
Enjoy your life to the fullest
Cherish every moment forever
That's what you are, my dears, my daughters.
