Unspoken Words

My heart is numb, yet aching to burst
Giving me a choking sensation
Suffocating with the echo of words
The melancholic echoes of forgotten whispers
The echoes from an unconditional love
Breaking at the very core of its centre.


Filled with soggy thoughts
Think twice-its harshness lingers
My soul stirred with winged emotions
Emotions unfurl from the darkest depths.


Murmurs gather, words take flight
But have no life, stuck on my lips
The precious words, still lie inside
Longing to come out
Ready to push gently
From the core of my heart-
The unspoken words
