Whispers of Eternity

The feeling of invisibility descends
A soothing touch of eternity enters
Befriending us as guide and lover
Revealing naked emotions and feelings.


It goes deep into the heart of the dear
Gently kissing with its mesmerizing hues
Daring to reach its zenith
Yet failing to meet the depth of emotions.


A tinge of blissful joy spreads far and wide
Never fading away, deeper inside
The tiniest thing makes us grand
Keeping us alive and happy.


It realizes with pain that no one cares;
The sublime emotions break like waves
It's pure, true and divine
Its divinity, no one can erase.


Somewhere hidden, yet found everywhere,
In words, in deeds and in feelings,
It serves as the bond of a noble spirit,
Eternal whispers from the sacred realm.
